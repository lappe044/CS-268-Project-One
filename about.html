Our Story

Epic MT started with two women, a love of nature, and the trip of a lifetime. After graduating college, our two co-founders, Maggie and Taylor, embarked on a cross continental backpacking trip comprising some of the most famous mountains and hiking trails in the world. Their life-changing journey inspired them to make backpacking more accessible to everyone which led to birth of EPIC MT.

Our Mission

At Epic MT, our mission is to make backpacking more accessible to everyone, from the experienced backpackers to the nervous newbie. That is why we provide you with everything you need for a successful backpacking trip: gear, food, routes, and an experienced guide. Additionally, our backpacking trips are available all over the world: even in Antarctica! From North America to Australia, Epic MT is here to provide you with an unforgettable backpacking experience.
